##### Introducing Medi AI

Welcome to Medi AI! I'm excited to share my latest project, a comprehensive AI-powered health assistant mobile application. This platform is designed to bring advanced health insights and medical guidance to your fingertips, with an intuitive interface and cutting-edge AI technology.

<div class="mt-6"></div>

##### What is Medi AI?

Medi AI is a mobile health assistant application that leverages artificial intelligence to provide personalized health insights, symptom analysis, medication management, and wellness tracking. Whether you're looking to understand symptoms, track your mood, manage medications, or get health-related answers, Medi AI aims to offer you data-driven resources to make informed health decisions.

<div class="mt-6"></div>

##### Key Features:

-   **AI-Powered Health Chat**: Engage in meaningful conversations with an AI assistant to get answers to your health questions, scan food items, and receive personalized health guidance.
-   **Symptom Checker**: Advanced symptom assessment tool that guides you through a comprehensive questionnaire to help identify potential health conditions and provide relevant medical information.
-   **Medication Reminders**: Never miss a dose with smart medication reminders and tracking. Set custom schedules and receive timely notifications.
-   **Mood Calendar**: Track your daily feelings and mood patterns over time with an intuitive calendar interface for better mental health awareness.
-   **Daily Health Tips**: Receive personalized daily health recommendations and tips based on current health trends and best practices.
-   **Multi-Language Support**: Available in multiple languages including English, Spanish, French, German, Italian, and Portuguese for global accessibility.
-   **Dark Mode**: Beautiful dark and light themes for comfortable viewing at any time of day.
-   **Subscription Model**: Flexible subscription tiers with RevenueCat integration for premium features and unlimited AI interactions.

<div class="mt-6"></div>

##### The Technology Behind Medi AI

<div class="mt-4"></div>

The application is built using a modern, cross-platform technology stack:

<div class="mt-2"></div>

-   **Frontend Framework**: Built with **React Native** and **Expo** for seamless cross-platform development, ensuring consistent performance on both iOS and Android.
-   **Language**: **TypeScript** for type-safe, maintainable code with enhanced developer experience.
-   **Navigation**: **Expo Router** for file-based routing and native navigation patterns.
-   **State Management**: **Zustand** for efficient and lightweight state management across the application.
-   **Authentication**: **Firebase Authentication** with support for Google Sign-In and Apple Authentication for secure user access.
-   **Backend Services**: Custom API integration for AI-powered health insights and symptom analysis.
-   **Subscriptions**: **RevenueCat** for managing in-app purchases and subscription tiers.
-   **Notifications**: **Expo Notifications** for medication reminders and daily health tips.
-   **Internationalization**: **i18next** and **react-i18next** for comprehensive multi-language support.
-   **UI Components**: Custom components built with React Native's core components, styled with StyleSheet for optimal performance.
-   **Crash Reporting**: **Firebase Crashlytics** for monitoring and improving app stability.
