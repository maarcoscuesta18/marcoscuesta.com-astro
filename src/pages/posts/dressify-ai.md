##### Introducing Outfit AI

Welcome to Outfit AI! I'm excited to share my latest project, a comprehensive AI-powered fashion assistant mobile application. This platform is designed to revolutionize how you discover, try on, and shop for outfits, with an intuitive interface and cutting-edge AI technology powered by Google's Gemini.

<div class="mt-6"></div>

##### What is Outfit AI?

Outfit AI (also known as Dressify) is a mobile fashion assistant application that leverages artificial intelligence to generate realistic outfit simulations, estimate body measurements for perfect fit, and help you shop for similar pieces from real retailers. Whether you're looking to visualize how an outfit would look on you, get personalized fit recommendations, or find shoppable items from inspiration photos, Outfit AI offers you AI-driven tools to make informed fashion decisions.

<div class="mt-6"></div>

##### Key Features:

-   **AI-Powered Outfit Generation**: Upload your photo and optional garment images to generate realistic outfit simulations using Google's Gemini 2.5 Flash Image model. See how different styles and pieces would look on you before making a purchase.
-   **Smart Measurements**: Advanced AI-powered body measurement tool that analyzes front and side photos to estimate your measurements, body shape, and provide personalized fit recommendations and tailoring suggestions.
-   **Shop the Look**: Upload inspiration photos from street style, social media, or anywhere else, and get AI-curated shoppable matches from real retailers in your region with pricing and availability information.
-   **Generation History**: Save and revisit all your generated outfits with timestamps, so you can compare looks and build your personal style library.
-   **Quick Style Templates**: Jump-start your creativity with pre-configured style templates and trend options for instant outfit generation.
-   **Multi-Language Support**: Available in multiple languages including English, Spanish, French, German, Italian, and Portuguese for global accessibility.
-   **Dark Mode**: Beautiful dark and light themes for comfortable viewing at any time of day.
-   **Subscription Model**: Flexible subscription tiers with RevenueCat integration for premium features and unlimited AI generations.

##### The Technology Behind Outfit AI

<div class="mt-4"></div>

The application is built using a modern, cross-platform technology stack:

<div class="mt-2"></div>

-   **Frontend Framework**: Built with **React Native** and **Expo** for seamless cross-platform development, ensuring consistent performance on both iOS and Android.
-   **Language**: **TypeScript** for type-safe, maintainable code with enhanced developer experience.
-   **Navigation**: **Expo Router** for file-based routing and native navigation patterns.
-   **State Management**: **Zustand** for efficient and lightweight state management across the application.
-   **AI & Image Generation**: **Google Gemini AI** (Gemini 2.5 Flash Image) for generating realistic outfit simulations and Gemini Vision for analyzing body measurements and outfit inspiration.
-   **Authentication**: **Firebase Authentication** with support for Google Sign-In, Apple Authentication, and Email/Password for secure user access.
-   **Backend Services**: **Firebase Firestore** for storing generation history and user data, and **Firebase Storage** for managing generated images.
-   **Subscriptions**: **RevenueCat** for managing in-app purchases and subscription tiers (weekly and lifetime plans).
-   **Internationalization**: **i18next** and **react-i18next** for comprehensive multi-language support.
-   **UI Components**: Custom components built with React Native's core components, styled with StyleSheet for optimal performance.
-   **Crash Reporting**: **Firebase Crashlytics** for monitoring and improving app stability.
-   **Image Processing**: **Expo Image Picker** and **Expo File System** for seamless photo capture and processing.

##### Key User Flows:

-   **Outfit Generation**: Upload your photo and optionally a garment image, choose a style or write a custom prompt, and generate realistic outfit simulations in seconds.
-   **Smart Measurements**: Complete a guided photo capture process (front and optional side view) to get AI-estimated body measurements, shape analysis, and personalized fit recommendations.
-   **Shop the Look**: Upload an inspiration photo, let AI detect key items, select focus areas, and receive curated shoppable matches from real retailers with pricing and direct links.
-   **Style Exploration**: Browse quick style options, trending styles, and templates to discover new outfit ideas and generate variations instantly.
